Property Description

Rare opportunity to own a stately retreat on over 10 acres within Jackson city limits. This private estate combines serenity and space with proximity to shopping, dining, and schools. The flexible layout supports an in-law suite or office conversion with its dedicated library. The chef's kitchen includes two Sub-Zero refrigerators, gas cooktop, granite counters, prep sink, and built-in workstation.
