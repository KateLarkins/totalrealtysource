Property Description

Welcome to "Oaklea Farm", where timeless architecture blends with unique potential...a rare offering of character, versatility, and future opportunity! Nestled on 22.5 scenic acres at the corner of College St & Industrial Blvd in Trenton, TN, this mid-century modern ranch is a rare gem! This 3BR/2BA home exudes timeless character with wood, stone, and brick construction, flagstone and hardwood floors, vaulted ceilings with exposed beams, and a double-sided masonry fireplace in the sunroom and den.

The circular drive leads to a 2-car carport with a built-in potting station featuring custom cabinetry, tiled workspace, and sink. Enjoy a beautifully remodeled kitchen (approx. 2005) with granite counters, custom cabinetry, dual sinks, built-in SubZero fridge, a separate Dacor cooktop. Outside, relax by the 20x42 in-ground pool (liner ~4 yrs old), entertain in the 36x20 pool house with kitchenette and wood stove (also has 2 full baths), or explore the picturesque pond and creek lined by split rail fencing.

The 36x20 pool house features two full baths, a kitchenette, and a spacious great room with a wood stove — offering incredible potential to convert into a loft-style guest house, studio, or ADU (Accessory Dwelling Unit) with minimal modification. Outbuildings include: 20x20 wired shop, 18x32 dairy barn, 48x78 loft barn, 22x80 implement shed, metal storage, 2 grain silos, and 2 pole barns—perfect for hobby farming or storage. With a mix of vintage charm and practical space, this one-of-a-kind property is a true legacy estate! The rear section along Industrial Blvd presents exceptional commercial real estate potential, making this property ideal for mixed-use development, hobby farming, or as a private estate. Priced at recent appraised value. Owner willing to subdivide.
